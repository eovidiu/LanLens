Privacy Policy for LanLens

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Overview

LanLens ("the App") is a network scanning utility developed by Ovidiu Eftimie. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

What We DO NOT Collect

LanLens does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No user accounts or registration
  • No cloud storage of your data
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No third-party data sharing

What Stays on Your Device

All data generated by LanLens remains exclusively on your local device:

  • Network scan results: Device IPs, MAC addresses, hostnames, and open ports discovered on your local network
  • Device fingerprints: DHCP fingerprints and TLS fingerprints used for device identification
  • User preferences: App settings and configurations
  • Cached data: Fingerprint database cache for offline device identification

This data is stored in the app's sandboxed container and is not accessible to other applications.

Network Access

LanLens requires local network access to perform its core functionality:

  • Local Network Discovery: The App scans your local network to discover connected devices
  • Port Scanning: The App probes devices on your network to identify open ports and services
  • Device Fingerprinting: The App may connect to devices on your network to gather identification data (e.g., UPnP queries, TLS handshakes)
Important: LanLens only communicates with devices on your local network. It does not send any data to external servers.

Optional Features

Fingerbank API (Optional)

If you choose to enable Fingerbank integration by providing an API key:

  • Your device MAC addresses may be sent to Fingerbank's servers for device identification
  • This is entirely optional and disabled by default
  • You can obtain your own API key from fingerbank.org

Local API Server (Optional)

LanLens can optionally run a local HTTP API server for integration with other applications. This server only listens on your local machine or local network, and no data is sent to external servers.

Data Retention

  • Scan data persists until you clear it within the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
  • No backups are made to external services

Children's Privacy

LanLens does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
